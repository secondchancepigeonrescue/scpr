Adopting is a lifelong commitment. Pigeons in captivity can live anywhere from 10 to 15 years on average, and many birds reach the extent of 20+ years! Our priority as a rescue is to ensure our birds are well cared for and that our adopters understand the commitment they're taking when they adopt, matching potential owners to birds so that both parties are happy!
