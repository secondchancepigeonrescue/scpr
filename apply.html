APPLY

Thank you for choosing to welcome a pigeon into your home! Second Chance Pigeon Rescue's goal at the end of every day is to adopt out our rescue birds into a loving home. We allow potential adopters to inquire about birds they're interested in, but will match you with the bird best for you at the end of the day. We offer shipping in proper weather conditions or meet up within two hours of Franklin, OH.
