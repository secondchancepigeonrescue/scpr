Thank you for choosing to welcome a pigeon into your home! Second Chance Pigeon Rescue allows adopters to inquire about birds they're interested in, but will match you with the bird best for you at the end of the day. Fostering provides a temporary home where a pigeon can rest and recover, adjusting while waiting for permanent placement. SCPR does allow foster-to-adopt for those not quite yet sure on if they can dedicate themselves to a pigeon long term. Adopting requires a lifelong commitment to these birds, which can range from now till the end of their ten to fifteen year lifespan, up to twenty or more years!

Requirements

• Must be 18 years old or older, or if a minor, have a parent/guardian apply for them
• Provide a valid photo ID
• Have stable housing and/or landlord approval
• Have pigeons housed securely in a predator-proofed area if applicable (outdoor or indoors with other pets)
• Agree to our no-breeding policy
• Agree that the bird cannot be sold, given away, or transferred
• Provide an appropriate enclosure, diet, and routine care
• Seek veterinary care when necessary
• Allow follow up communication
• In addition, fosterers must communicate regularly with updates, transport to veterinary appointments, understand that the bird remains property of Second Chance Pigeon Rescue, and never rehome or transfer the bird independently.

We reserve the right to deny any application that does not meet our placement standards.



Adoption Process

Adoption Application Adoption Contract Health Disclosure Agreement

First, submit your application. This will include details such as your name, address, and living situation. We do not allow anyone under the age of 18 to adopt a bird from Second Chance Pigeon Rescue. A minor attempting to adopt must apply via a parent or guardian who will assume responsibility over the bird.

After we review your application, we'll send you an email for follow up questions. Adoption application response time varies, but will be about a week. Look out for an email from secondchancepigeonrescue@gmail.com. We do not do "first-come-first-serve." Placements are based on pigeon compatability with appliers.

If you're approved to move forward, we discuss care expectations in depth. This includes housing set ups, diet, enrichment, free roam time if applicable, and more, to ensure you understand the level of dedication it takes to care for a pigeon. If all requirements are met and agreed upon, you'll sign the adoption contract and health disclosure agreement, pay the adoption fee, and then match you to a bird or birds!

After matching you to a bird, we can decide on deliver method. We deliver pigeons personally within a two hour driving distance of Franklin, OH, and offer shipping or meet up anywhere further. We do not ship in inclement weather.



Foster Process

We do not currently have a foster program officially in place. As we grow, we will update!
